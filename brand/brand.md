# IdentityShift.io — Brand Reference

## Colors

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Black | `#0B0B0D` | Primary background |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Text on dark, alt backgrounds |
| Light Gray | `#F7F7F8` | Light section backgrounds |
| Accent (warm) | `#FFB000` | Key actions, highlights — use sparingly |
| Accent (deep) | `#FF8A00` | Gradient end, hover states |

Accent gradient: `#FFB000 → #FF8A00` (135deg)

The logo splits the wordmark: "Identity" white/light + "**Shift**" in accent orange + ".io" white/light.

## Typography

- **Primary font:** Inter (all weights)
- **Headlines:** Bold, large, dominant — let them breathe
- **Body:** Regular weight, clean, readable
- Load via: `https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Inter:wght@400;600;700;800&display=swap`

## Logo

- Wordmark: `IdentityShift.io`
- "Identity" — light weight
- "Shift" — bold, accent orange (#FFB000)
- ".io" — light weight
- Dark version (white on black): `brand/assets/logo-dark.png`
- Light version (dark on white): `brand/assets/logo-light.png`

## Visual Style

- Clean, Apple-like simplicity
- Dark canvas primary; alternating dark/white sections
- Accent color used sparingly — only on key moments/CTAs
- No clutter; let space do the work

## Motion Guidelines (for Hyperframes)

- Reveal "Shift" in accent orange as a beat — it's the brand's emotional payload
- Favor smooth/dramatic easing over bouncy
- Dark background is home base; light backgrounds for contrast moments
- Accent gradient works well on text glows and underline reveals
- Keep compositions minimal — one idea per scene

## Brand Voice (for lower-thirds / captions)

- Smart. Direct. Slightly contrarian.
- No fluff, no hype
- Hooks: pattern interrupt → relatable truth → reframe → soft close
- Tone: "slightly sarcastic truth"
- Example lines:
  - "If mindset worked, you wouldn't still be starting over."
  - "You don't need more motivation. You need a system."
  - "Stop starting over. Start becoming."

## Taglines

- Your habits aren't the problem. Your identity is.
- Stop starting over. Start becoming.
- Change your actions. Your identity follows.
- Science-backed identity change.
